About Our Gym

FitLife Bangladesh offers state-of-the-art equipment, professional trainers, and a variety of fitness classes to help you achieve your health goals. Our management system allows you to register for classes, track your attendance, manage payments, and explore our course offerings all in one place.

We're proud to serve fitness enthusiasts across Bangladesh with locations in Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, and Khulna.

Our Features

  • Easy online registration and login
  • Comprehensive class schedules with filtering options
  • Professional Bangladeshi trainer profiles and specialties
  • Digital attendance tracking system
  • Secure payment processing in Bangladeshi Taka (BDT)
  • Diverse course catalog with detailed descriptions
